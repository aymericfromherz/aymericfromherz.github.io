Aymeric Fromherz

Hi! I'm currently a researcher in the Prosecco Team at Inria Paris.

My research focuses on how to design and build secure systems, using automated techniques such as symbolic execution and abstract interpretation, as well as formal methods. I'm currently a member of the Everest Project.

Previously, I was a PhD student in Cylab at Carnegie Mellon University, coadvised by Bryan Parno and Corina Pasareanu. Before joining CMU, I received my Bachelor's and Master's degree from the Ecole Normale Superieure in Paris, France.
